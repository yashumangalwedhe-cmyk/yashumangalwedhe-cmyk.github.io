Vibrating Conveyor — Redesign & Analysis
Full structural and dynamic analysis of a food-grade vibrating conveyor for chicken nugget processing. Performed FEM simulations in SolidWorks to analyse beam deflection, buckling loads, and natural frequencies. Identified design failures — amplitude 93% below requirement, frequency ratio causing resonance risk — and redesigned the system to meet all client specifications including 30mm amplitude, max 2g acceleration, and frequency ratio ≥ 3. Achieved deflection of 0.17mm against a 1mm requirement.
